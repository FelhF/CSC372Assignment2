Travelogue: Paris

destination

Overview

Paris, the city of love and glamour. A city with one of the most well known world attractions, the Eiffel Tower. Come enjoy our traditional cuisine in its purest state.

Must see locations in Paris in importance order.

  1. Eiffel Tower
  2. Louvre Museum
  3. Notre Dame
  4. Seine River Guided Cruise
  5. Versailles Palace and Gardens Tour

These are some tips for first time visitors:

  • Buy your tickets ahead of time. Otherwise, you might miss out on soom of the attractions.
  • Make sure to bring your important documents with you and always keep an eye for them.
  • Some basic knowledge of French or a means to translate can greatly improve your experience.
  • While in a tour, do not get separated from your group.

Here is a testimonial from a recent tourist:

"I will be traveling to a some more countries this year... It might be hard for those countries to compete with what I enjoyed in Paris."
